About Us

TNMOA is here to fight for the rights and freedom of Medical officers.
The New Era has begun for the betterment of the Oppressed Public
health doctors.

Our Vision

Empowering Public Health Doctors

Our Mission

We request for doctor's benefits, we represent for doctor's issues, we fight for doctor's Rights.

We bring "Suitable Stable Income, Assured Career Growth, Safe Work Environment."

Our Short-Term Agends

    We will fight for the maximum benefits for medical fraternity in SEVENTH PAY COMMISSION

    Channelizing the individual benefits for the doctors like DA, TA, EL surrender, Medical leave, Maternity Leave without delay.

    Getting all doctors post at DDHS office as a sanctioned post to improve monitory methods in the community. .

    Power delegations to BMO and INCHARGE MO. Getting sanctioned post for BMO and designated post for MO. This will empower the public health system forever.

    Transfer of service from Medical officer to Health officer to infuse good administrators in the public health.
